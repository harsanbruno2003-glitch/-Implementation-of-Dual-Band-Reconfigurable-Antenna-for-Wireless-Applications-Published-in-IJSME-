# 📡 Dual-Band Reconfigurable Flexible Antenna for Wireless Applications

A published research project from my electronics & communication engineering background — designing a flexible antenna that can dynamically switch and tune across two frequency bands.

![Antenna Design](https://img.shields.io/badge/RF-Antenna_Design-blue?style=flat)
![Published](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Published-success?style=flat)

## 📌 Overview
Modern wireless devices need antennas that adapt rather than stay fixed to one frequency — think IoT devices, mobile handsets, satellite links, wireless sensor networks, and 5G infrastructure all sharing the same physical hardware. This project designs and implements a **dual-band reconfigurable flexible antenna** capable of dynamic frequency switching and tuning, addressing that demand for versatility in a single compact form factor.

## 🏆 Publication
- Accepted at the **2024 7th International Conference on Devices, Circuits and Systems (ICDCS)**
- Acceptance letter from **IJISME** (International Journal of Innovative Science, Engineering & Management) included in this repo

## 🔍 Scope
- Dual-band design enabling operation across two distinct frequency ranges
- Flexible substrate construction for conformal/wearable applications
- Reconfigurability for dynamic frequency switching and tuning
- Target applications: mobile devices, IoT, satellite communication, wireless sensor networks, 5G
